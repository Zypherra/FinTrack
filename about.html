About Us

FinTrack is a personal finance management application that helps you track your income and expenses, set budgets, and achieve your financial goals.

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their finances and achieve financial freedom.

Our team is passionate about personal finance and dedicated to providing users with the tools and resources they need to succeed.

We believe that everyone deserves to have control over their finances and achieve their financial goals.
